Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Sprinkler World (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share personal information when you use our website, request estimates or callbacks, or engage our irrigation services in California.

Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Contact details you provide (name, phone number, email address, service address).
  • Project details related to irrigation design, installation, repair, or water-efficiency upgrades.
  • Communications you send us by phone, email, or web forms.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type, and general location derived from IP address, used to secure and improve the site.

How we use information

We use personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries, schedule visits, and provide proposals or invoices.
  • Perform irrigation and landscape water-management services you request.
  • Comply with applicable laws, including water-use reporting or documentation required by local agencies when relevant to your project.
  • Improve our website and customer support.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who assist our business (for example, email or scheduling tools) under confidentiality terms, or when required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our customers and employees.

California residents

If you are a California resident, you may have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion of certain information subject to exceptions, and to opt out of “sale” or “sharing” of personal information as those terms are defined by law. We do not sell personal information in the conventional sense. To exercise applicable rights, contact us using the information below. We will verify reasonable requests as required by law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information we handle. No online transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use of our site or services after changes means you accept the updated policy.
