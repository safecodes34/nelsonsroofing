Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

Nelson's Roofing (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share personal information when you use our website, request estimates or callbacks, or engage our roofing services in Westchester County, New York, and nearby areas we serve.

Information we collect

We may collect:

  • Contact details you provide (name, phone number, email address, property address).
  • Project details related to roof inspections, repairs, replacements, storm damage, or maintenance.
  • Communications you send us by phone, email, or web forms.
  • Technical data such as browser type, device type, and general location derived from IP address, used to secure and improve the site.

How we use information

We use personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries, schedule visits, and provide proposals or invoices.
  • Perform roofing and related exterior services you request.
  • Comply with applicable laws and cooperate with insurers when you ask us to support a claim.
  • Improve our website and customer support.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers who assist our business (for example, email or scheduling tools) under confidentiality terms, or when required by law or to protect our rights and the safety of our customers and employees.

New York and other U.S. residents

Depending on where you live, federal or state law may provide you with additional privacy rights (for example, the right to access or delete certain information we hold). Laws vary by jurisdiction and may not apply to all information we collect in a business context. To ask questions or exercise rights that may apply to you, contact us using the information below. We will respond to reasonable requests as required by applicable law.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information we handle. No online transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date will change when we do. Continued use of our site or services after changes means you accept the updated policy.
