Gable roofs

  • We inspect decking, flashings, and penetrations, not only the visible shingles, so repairs address why water got in, not just where it showed up indoors.
  • Tear-offs stay contained: tarps, magnetic sweep, and daily cleanup so nails and debris do not surprise kids, pets, or neighbors.
  • You get a written scope with layers (ice and water shield, underlayment, starter, cap) spelled out before we order material, so the roof you pay for matches the roof we install.
  • TPO, PVC, and modified assemblies are matched to slope, traffic, and warranty goals, not a one-membrane-fits-all shortcut over wet insulation.
  • Parapets, scuppers, drains, and edge metal get sequencing and tie-ins that move water off the deck instead of into fascia, soffits, or top-floor rooms.
  • Skylights and penetrations on low slope get curbs, target patches, and compatible flashings detailed to manufacturer specs so details do not void coverage.
  • Clay and concrete profiles need correct underlayment, fastening, and batten layout; we follow manufacturer tables for wind and exposure in Westchester.
  • Valleys, hips, and ridges on tile roofs are leak-prone when rushed; we rebuild transitions and bird stops so water sheds predictably in wind-driven rain.
  • When sections are repairable, we match existing modules and color lots where possible and document what was replaced so future service is straightforward.

FAQ

Common questions

How long does a roof replacement take?

Many residential re-roofs take two to five days depending on size, layers to remove, weather, and detail work like skylights or carpentry repairs.

Can you repair instead of replacing?

Yes, when decking is sound and the system still has service life. We will tell you plainly if a repair is a short-term patch versus a replacement that saves money over time.

What warranties do you offer?

Manufacturer warranties cover materials per their programs. Any workmanship warranty we provide is written on your proposal; ask us to walk through what is covered before work starts.

What areas do you serve?

We serve homes and businesses throughout Westchester County, New York.
